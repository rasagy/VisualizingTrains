How do the 4 metros connect India?

Visualizing trains from the four Indian metros: Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai & Kolkata.

The map starts with the shortest routes first, and shows longer routes with time.

Hover on the lines to know more about the particular train & connecting stations.


To know more about the process, or to share feedback/suggestions, head to my blog post!
